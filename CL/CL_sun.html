🧠 QUICK MEMORY TRICKS
Lock these in before you start — your future self will thank you.
✍️ E6.1 Story Openings: The best openings drop the reader straight into action or mystery. Avoid starting with "I am going to tell you about..." — start with a moment that makes the reader ask: what happens next?
🎭 E6.2 Show Don't Tell: Instead of "she was nervous," SHOW it — "her hands shook." Instead of "it was cold," SHOW it — "frost crept across the window." Examiners reward specific, sensory details.

⚠️ WATCH OUT FOR THESE▼
E5.2 Inference: Don't just find exact words in the text — ask yourself "What is the author hinting at?" The answer is always a deduction, not a quotation.
M2.1 Percentages: Watch out — percent means out of 100, not just any division. 25 percent of 60: divide by 100 to get 0.6, then times 25 = 15.
E3.1 Commas: After a fronted adverbial (starts with After, Before, When, Although), you must put a comma before the main clause.

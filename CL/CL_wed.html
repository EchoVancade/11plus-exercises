🏆 PRO TIPS FOR TODAY▼
E5.2 Inference: The answer is NEVER copied straight from the text — it is your deduction. Look for clues (what characters do, say, or avoid saying) and work out the hidden meaning.
M2.1 Percentages: Pro move — divide by 100 first to get 1 percent, then multiply. Example: 35 percent of 80. Step 1: 80 divided by 100 = 0.8. Step 2: 0.8 times 35 = 28.
E3.1 Commas: Always put a comma after a fronted adverbial. If the sentence starts with Although, When, After, Before, Despite — there is a comma before the main clause.

🏕️ Mission Pack M-B
The Quiet Setting
A character arrives somewhere empty and still. Use all 3 techniques below.
1
Sensory Detail (W2.6): Describe what you smell or feel — not just what you see. Go beyond the obvious.
2
Expanded Noun Phrases (W4.2): Use at least 2 detailed noun phrases. Example: "the cold, crumbling stone archway" not just "the archway".
3
Time Passing (W1.2): Slow a moment right down — use short sentences to stretch out what happens in just a few seconds.

🧠 QUICK MEMORY TRICKS
Think about these before you start writing!
✍️ Good story openings: Don't start with "Once upon a time" or "My name is..." Try to start in the middle of something exciting! Drop the reader straight into the action.
🌟 Describing things: Use your senses! What can you SEE? HEAR? FEEL? Instead of saying "it was dark," try "shadows crept across the walls like silent ghosts."

✏️ Sentence Starters — Write these on paper!
1
As the door swung open...
What did you see? What sounds did you hear?
2
The first thing I noticed was...
Use your senses — what did you see, smell, or feel?
3
It felt like [simile — use like or as]...
E.g. "It felt like stepping into another world."
4
I decided to step inside, even though...
Show the reader what was going through your mind!
5
I will never forget the day...
This is your ending — make it memorable!
📝 Write your sentences on paper and show a grown-up!

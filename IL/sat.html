Ember the Baking Dragon

Deep in the Cinnamon Hills, there lived a dragon named Ember. Unlike other dragons, Ember did not breathe fire — she breathed warm cinnamon-scented air, which was perfect for baking.


Every morning, Ember shaped ginger biscuits from a huge bowl of dough, then huffed gently over them to bake them golden. The whole hillside smelled wonderful.


One afternoon, a traveller stumbled into her cave. He was cold and frightened, and his teeth were chattering.


"A dragon!" he gasped, pressing himself against the wall.


Ember blinked slowly. Then she gave him a smoky smile and set a cup of chamomile tea and a warm ginger biscuit on the stone beside him.


The traveller stared. He looked at the biscuit. He looked at the dragon. Slowly, he reached out and took a bite.


"Oh," he said. "Oh, that's wonderful."


Ember puffed a small cloud of cinnamon smoke and went back to her baking.

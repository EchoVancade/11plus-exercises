📖 Max Finds a Treasure

Max the dog loved the park. One sunny morning, Max was digging near the big oak tree when his paw hit something hard. He barked loudly and his owner, Sam, came running.

Sam knelt down and brushed away the soil with her hands. Underneath was a small tin box. It was rusty and old, with a picture of a ship on the lid.

Sam opened the box carefully. Inside were five old coins and a tiny piece of paper with a drawing on it.

"It's a map!" said Sam.

Max wagged his tail and jumped up, barking happily. He did not know what a map was, but Sam's excitement was catching.
